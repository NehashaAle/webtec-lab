General Background

Sky International College and Research Center Private Limited is registered with the objective of running management programs Sky International College and Research Center (SICRC) in Tilottama Municipality of Lumbini Province.

  • Mission

    • Our mission is to create capable manpower in the field of management by employing the best possible teaching approaches and methods designed to promote enduring knowledge and an aspiration to serve the motherland, where students' happiness and wellbeing are valued. We treat all our teaching and non-teaching staff as human capital anda are committed to their growth. Transparency in all our financial transactions and ethical practices are our core values.

  • Vision

    • Our vision is to be a premier management college in the country, remembered for producing capable, ethical, responsible, patriotic and spritually grounded graduates.

  • Objective
  • To prepare graduates as enterpreneurs.
  • To nurture students so that none of them remain unemployed.
  • To contribute to innovation and applicable research.
  • To reduce the need for students to pursue namesake studies abroad.
